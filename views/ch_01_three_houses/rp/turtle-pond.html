Dimitri Alexandre Blaiddyd BOT 02-Mar-20 02:24 PM
What exactly was The Archbishop trying to accomplish with such...frivolities? Dimitri sat at the foot of the pond, his reflection peering back at him as the strange creatures poked their heads in and out of the water. Their beady little eyes staring back at him every so often. It was strange, yes, but he could see the appeal after a while. Some company, human or not, was always appreciated. But just why? Would the company of the animals truly help with the upcoming test? Dimitri sighed, leaning back as the animals continued to laze about. Should I….really be spending my time here…. The battle was creeping up on them by the minute. As the realization started to seep in, Dimitri stood back up, waving rather awkwardly at the rippling water. “I...better be going now. Some more training wouldn’t hurt, would it? Well, goodbye!” The view was almost pathetic, the prince expecting a response from something that mindlessly peered up for food. With another silent sigh, he made his way for the exit. That is until a loud thud sent him toppling back. He winced a little before he realized what he had done. “Gh-! I….Ah! I'm so sorry!”
